For Schools

What should you know before applying?

Research study on "Positive Peace Education" explores the possibilities for culturally relevant Positive Peace Education in Kazakhstan. Participants of the project will be invited to join several days of intensive workshops that intend to develop understanding of peace in school context, introducing Positive Peace and facilitating integration of Peace in schools. This research is undertaken in collaboration with Nazarbayev University Graduate School of Education (NUGSE), the University of Cambridge, and the National Academy of Education named after Altynsarin (NAE). The PI of the project is Lynne Parmenter, professor, NUGSE; Co-PIs of the project are Hilary Cremin, dean, Faculty of Education at Cambridge; Madina Tynybayeva, President of NAE; and Aida Akhmetzhanova, vice President of NAE.


What are we expecting from you?

In this project, the team of school principal and teachers will be asked to:
participate in workshops on “Facilitating Positive Peace Education”, "Action Research", "Ethics"
conduct action research in your school site
bridge the connections with students, parents and research team
participate in semi-structured interviews
share project outcomes related to Peace Facilitation in your school.

Pilot School: Binom

BINOM SCHOOL is a unique project in the field of education, implemented through the mechanism of public-private partnership between the innovative construction holding BI Group and the akimat of Astana. Education in schools is based on the principle of Amanat - respect, care, responsibility, diligence, improvement based on the works of the great orientalist philosopher Abu Nasr al-Farabi.
Each BINOM school is a 5-storey building with 127 classrooms, 9 advanced laboratories in physics, chemistry, biology, nano- and biotechnologies.
